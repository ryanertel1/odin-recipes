Fettuccine Alfredo

Picture the first italian food you can think of, is it fettuccine alfredo? Yeah, i knew it would be, nobody with real taste thinks of spaghetti first. Now that we know we're in good company let's get into the recipe.

Ingredients (4 servings):

  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot heat water to boiling. Add salt to water.
  2. Add fettuccine to boiling water
  3. In large skillet heat butter over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in heavy cream
  5. Let cream reduce and cook for 5 to 8 minutes. Add half of the parmesan cheese to the mixture and whisk well until smooth. Keep over heat and whisk well until cheese is melted
  6. Drain water from pasta, but save some pasta water for later
  7. Toss the sauce with the pasta and add half of the parmesan cheese. Toss the pasta and add the rest of the parmesan and a little pasta water if needed to thin the sauce.
