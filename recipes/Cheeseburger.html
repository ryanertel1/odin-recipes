Cheeseburger

Everybody loves a good cheeseburger, it's one of the staple foods of the american diet. It contains all the cornerstones of good comfort food: cheese, red meat, and carbs

Ingredients (3 servings):

  • 3 burger buns
  • 1 pound 80/20 beef
  • 3 slices cheese of choice (american/cheddar/other)
  • Any desired topping (ex:lettuce/tomato/onion)

Instructions:

  1. Seperate beef into 1/3 pound balls and form into patties
  2. Season both sides of the patties generously with salt and black pepper
  3. Use two fingers or the back of a spoon to create a divot in the middle of the patties - This helps prevent the patties from rising in the center while cooking
  4. Add patties to a large pan on medium heat
  5. Cook patties until almost done to desired throughness (ex: Medium/Rare)
  6. Add cheese to top of patties and cover skillet until cheese melts
  7. Remove patties from heat and assemble burger with desired toppings

    8. Return to home
