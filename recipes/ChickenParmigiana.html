Chicken Parmigiana

Picture the first italian food you can think of, is it chicken parmigiana? Yeah, i knew it would be, nobody with real taste thinks of spaghetti first. Now that we know we're in good company let's get into the recipe.

Ingredients (4 servings):

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • vegetable or pure olive oil
  • Tomato sauce
  • 1 pound thinly slices mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Pound chicken breasts thin and season both sides with salt and pepper
  3. mix flour with salt and black pepper
  4. beat eggs with 2 tablespoons water and season with salt and pepper
  5. dredge chicken breasts in flour, then dip in egg wash, and then dredge in bread crumbs
  6. heat large sauce pan with oil until almost smoking. Add chicken breasts to pan and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side.
  7. Put chicken breasts on baking sheet and top with tomato sauce, a few slices of mozzarella, salt and pepper, and a tablespoon of parmesan.
  8. Bake chicken in the oven until cooked through and cheese melted, about 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from oven and let settle before eating.
